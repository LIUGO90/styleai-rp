Privacy Policy for Styla

Last updated: January 6, 2025

Introduction

Styla ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your name (if you choose to share it) and email address
  • Profile Information: Photos you upload for styling analysis (outfit photos, clothing items, full-body shots)
  • Style Preferences: Your selected style preferences, body type information, and fashion choices

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features you use
  • Photos: Images you upload are temporarily stored for AI analysis

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized fashion advice and styling recommendations
  • Analyze your photos using AI to create outfit suggestions
  • Remember your style preferences and wardrobe
  • Improve our AI styling service
  • Communicate with you about the app

Data Storage and Security

  • Your photos are securely transmitted to our servers for AI processing
  • We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data
  • Your data is stored on secure cloud servers
  • We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign In: For authentication (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • AI Processing Services: For generating style recommendations
  • Cloud Storage: For secure data storage

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Update your information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us or use the "Clear All Data" option in the app settings.

Children's Privacy

Styla is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
